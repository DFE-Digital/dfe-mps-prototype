Claim a payment for teaching maths or physics

Claim £2,000 after tax if you:

  • teach maths or physics
  • have a degree or teaching qualification in maths or physics
  • have been teaching for 1 to 5 years
  • teach in certain places - check which places are eligible

The application takes around 5 to 15 minutes to complete.

Before you apply

You'll need:

  • your National Insurance Number
  • your bank account details
  • your 7 digit teacher reference number – you can get this from your school, the certificate you got when you qualified, or from the teacher qualifications helpdesk
  • the date you completed your initial teacher training (ITT)
  • sign in details for ‘Verify’ – the government’s secure way of proving who you are, if you do not have a Verify account, you’ll need your passport or photocard driving licence and a debit or credit card to sign up
