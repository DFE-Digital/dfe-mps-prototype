Claim a payment for teaching mathematics or physics

The Mathematics and Physics Teacher Retention Pilot is for mathematics and physics teachers who have a degree or teaching qualification in their subject and are in years 1 to 5 of their teaching career.

All eligible teachers will receive a £2,000 payment after tax if teaching in the North East, Yorkshire and the Humber, and remaining Opportunity Areas.

The application takes around 5 to 15 minutes to complete.

Before you apply

You will need:

  • your National Insurance Number
  • your bank account details
  • your 7 digit teacher reference number – you can get this from your school, the certificate you got when you qualified as a teacher or from the teacher qualifications helpdesk
  • the date you completed your Initial Teacher Training (ITT)
  • sign in details for ‘Verify’ (the government’s secure way of proving who you are) – if you do not have a Verify account, you will need your passport or photocard driving licence to sign up
